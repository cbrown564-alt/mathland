## Observation-to-retest matrix

| Observation | Product hypothesis | Implemented response | Retest measure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Editorial scale displaced mathematical work. | Compact orientation will reduce search and keep attention on the object. | Routine headings are capped and shortened; the Studio instrument appears immediately after one compact task frame. | Learner identifies the current move and begins manipulating without facilitator direction; object is visible in the first 768 px viewport. |
| Horizon choice and persistence were not self-explanatory. | Plain-language, journey-level horizon controls will make route behavior predictable. | Entry explains “choose once”; the header and Atlas name the active horizon; deliberate edits preserve evidence, detours, and return state. | Learner explains what the horizon changes, edits it intentionally, and returns without losing work. |
| Manipulation and calculation felt underpowered. | Comparison, extremes, exact state, history, and linked views will turn movement into an investigation. | Vector Studio adds four extreme cases, exact component input, pinned comparison, reset/undo, and linked geometric/component/symbolic/contextual views. Calculation collects both signed contributions and the net. | Learner forms and tests a directional claim, uses at least two control paths, and explains how two linked views agree. |
| Incorrect answers repeated a correctness gate. | Preserving work and escalating specific help will support recovery without disguising dependence. | Recovery sequence: observation, cue, comparison, worked step, diagnostic detour, fresh equivalent attempt. Support level is recorded; independent evidence stays open. | Learner revises the original attempt, states the changed reasoning, and understands the evidence label. |
| Finance transfer surprised or overloaded some learners. | Explicit translation will separate domain literacy from structural transfer. | Orientation names the reason for switching, stable operation, new terms and units, and simplifying boundaries. Finance may be deferred without transfer evidence. | Learner states what stayed fixed and what changed; deferment is understood and does not trap progress. |
| Climate retrieval risked testing vocabulary. | Domain orientation and equivalent substitution will protect the retrieval construct. | Climate model is introduced before the attempt; cues are staged; a quality-control context can substitute; previews do not count as delayed evidence. | Learner can restate the model before attempting; facilitator records whether vocabulary, retrieval, or arithmetic blocked access. |
| Atlas–Studio–Observatory was unfamiliar. | A miniature first-run Studio will teach the product through use. | Six-move, skippable, reopenable tour demonstrates prediction, manipulation, harmless error, help, evidence, and Atlas orientation. | Without explanation, learner completes or skips, later reopens Help, and identifies Atlas and Studio responsibilities. |
| Missing specialists and media reduced teaching range. | One bounded field lens can improve noticing without creating decorative character coverage. | Vera’s projection lens pairs a mathematical diagram, optional audio, transcript, and complete non-media path. | Learner names the mathematical feature the intervention clarified; record whether audio, diagram, transcript, or non-media path was used. |
| Reduced exposition risked reduced depth. | A visible formal spine can make insight portable without returning to exposition-first delivery. | Component definition, geometric identity, law-of-cosines derivation, sign consequence, model assumptions, and worked reasoning sit beside practice. | Learner connects the component and geometric definitions and can use the sign result beyond interface wording. |

## Adversarial integrity review response

The second review accepted the investigation-tool improvements but rejected the claim that the build yet tested learning “one operation across three worlds.” It also found mismatched support metadata, cheap Studio unlock, missing geometry-to-components work, a non-semantic two-dimensional slider role, static projection, ignored case vectors, conflicting Vera audio, decorative Atlas nodes, horizontal progress overflow, a retrieval exit trap, and one non-diagnostic repair worksheet.

The integrated research build now addresses those implementation findings: each Observatory system asks what its scalar measures; horizon choice loads real case vectors, labels, units, boundaries, and meanings; all three sign regions are required; live projection and both vector handles share keyboard, numeric, pointer, touch-capture-release, and readable paths; Practice includes geometry-to-components and explicit explanation criteria; support metadata matches observation, cue, comparison, worked, restudied, and fresh independent attempt; AI normalisation is practised; finance remains an oriented transfer; Vera's speech and transcript teach engineering projection; four repair types diagnose the blocked move; Atlas nodes for implemented Studios are operable; progression states explain their requirements; and retrieval never traps exit.

This closes accepted implementation issues, not the learner gate. No participant-level retest, seven-day retrieval result, assistive-technology review, or external engineering/ML/finance sign-off is present in the repository yet.
