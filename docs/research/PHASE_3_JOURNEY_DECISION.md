## Architecture consequences

- Engineering, AI, and finance remain lenses across shared territory IDs.
- Changing the horizon changes case data, labels, units, and route emphasis without discarding evidence or current work.
- Production promotion is bounded to dot product, normalisation, and projection Studios plus the diagnostic, transfer, retrieval, and Atlas paths already validated.
- Matrices, gradients, broad curriculum migration, cloud sync, and account systems remain unpromoted.
- Domain review remains a per-case content-operation requirement after production launch.
